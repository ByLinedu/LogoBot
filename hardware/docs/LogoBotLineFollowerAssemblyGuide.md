### Assembly Steps

1. Plug the Pro Mini Clips into the base and then snap the Arduino Pro Mini into them
![](../assemblies/LogoBotLineFollower/FinalAssembly_step1_view.png)
2. Pin the line sensor assembly to the underside
![](../assemblies/LogoBotLineFollower/FinalAssembly_step2_view.png)
4. Plug the two wheels assemblies into the base
![](../assemblies/LogoBotLineFollower/FinalAssembly_step4_view.png)
5. Connect the jumper wires between the motor drivers and the Arduino
![](../assemblies/LogoBotLineFollower/FinalAssembly_step5_view.png)
![](../assemblies/LogoBotLineFollower/FinalAssembly_step5_plan.png)
6. Fix the power toggle switch into place
![](../assemblies/LogoBotLineFollower/FinalAssembly_step6_view.png)
9. Align the caster assembly with the base, then insert a short pin to lock it to the base
![](../assemblies/LogoBotLineFollower/FinalAssembly_step9_view.png)
11. Attach the battery pack with self-adhesive hook and loop tape
![](../assemblies/LogoBotLineFollower/FinalAssembly_step11_view.png)
12. Wire in the terminal block to distribute power
![](../assemblies/LogoBotLineFollower/FinalAssembly_step12_view.png)
13. Wire up power to the stepper drivers and Arduino.  Then connect the microswitches to the Arduino.
![](../assemblies/LogoBotLineFollower/FinalAssembly_step13_view.png)
